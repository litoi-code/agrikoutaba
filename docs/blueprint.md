# **App Name**: AgriCentral

## Core Features:

- Customer & Supplier Management: Maintain profiles, contact details, and transaction history for both customers and suppliers.
- Inventory Tracking: Manage stock levels for seeds, fertilizers, and equipment, including reorder level tracking.
- Income & Expense Recording: Record income (sales, services) and expenses (payroll, operations) for financial oversight.
- Task Assignment & Tracking: Assign tasks (delivery, harvest, maintenance) to workers (field workers, supervisors) and track progress.
- Financial Forecasting Tool: AI tool which predicts cash flow using sales forecasts and pending expenses.
- Investment & Equity Tracking: Manage records of all investments, profit/loss, and equity details in the business.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23) to evoke nature and growth.
- Background color: Desaturated beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm brown (#A0522D) for highlights and key actions.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern and readable feel.
- Use simple, consistent icons representing different aspects of farming, like crops, tools, and weather.
- Dashboard-centric design providing quick access to key metrics and recent activities.
- Subtle animations when updating stock levels or completing tasks, providing visual feedback.
